# Laddu

Laddu is a popular Indian sweet made from flour, sugar, and ghee, shaped into small, round balls. Often flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts, it's a favorite treat for festivals and celebrations.

# Jelabi

Jalebi is a popular Indian dessert made by deep-frying swirls of fermented batter and soaking them in saffron-infused sugar syrup. Known for its crispy texture and sticky sweetness, jalebi is a festive treat enjoyed warm or cold.

# Paalcova

Paalcova, also known as milk peda, is a rich South Indian sweet made by slowly simmering milk until it thickens, then sweetened with sugar. With its creamy texture and melt-in-the-mouth flavor, it’s a popular treat during festivals and special occasions.

# Soan Papadi

Soan Papdi is a traditional Indian sweet with a flaky, melt-in-the-mouth texture, made from gram flour, sugar, and ghee. Often garnished with pistachios and cardamom, it’s a delicate treat enjoyed during festivals and celebrations.

# Mysore Pak

Mysore Pak is a rich, melt-in-the-mouth South Indian sweet made from a combination of gram flour, ghee, and sugar. Known for its soft, crumbly texture and aromatic flavor, it is a favorite during festivals and special occasions.

# Golab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a popular Indian dessert made of deep-fried dough balls soaked in a fragrant sugar syrup infused with rose water or cardamom. Soft, spongy, and sweet, it's a beloved treat at weddings, festivals, and celebrations.
