# Fish Fry

Fish fry is a delicious dish where fish fillets are coated in seasoned batter or spices and fried until crispy and golden. Often served with lemon wedges and dipping sauces, it’s a favorite for its flaky texture and rich flavor.

# Ramen

Ramen is a beloved Japanese noodle soup, featuring chewy noodles in a savory broth, often topped with ingredients like sliced pork, green onions, and a soft-boiled egg. Its rich flavors and comforting warmth make it a global favorite.

# Eggs

Eggs are a versatile and nutritious food, rich in protein and essential vitamins, commonly enjoyed in dishes from breakfast to dessert. Whether boiled, scrambled, or baked, they add flavor and texture to countless recipes worldwide.

# Salad

Salad is a fresh and healthy dish, typically combining leafy greens, vegetables, and a variety of toppings like fruits, nuts, and cheeses. With endless customization options, it's a delicious way to pack in nutrients and vibrant flavors.

# Fries

Fries are a crispy, golden snack made from thinly sliced potatoes, fried to perfection and often enjoyed with salt or various dips. A popular side dish, they bring a satisfying crunch and flavor to meals around the world.

# Burger

A burger is a hearty sandwich featuring a juicy patty, often made of beef, chicken, or plant-based ingredients, tucked in a soft bun with toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, and sauces. Loved globally, it’s a go-to comfort food with endless flavor variations.
